Meet Our Animatronic Characters

Freddy Fazbear

Freddy Fazbear is the iconic animatronic character at Freddy Fazbear's Pizza. Known for his friendly demeanor, Freddy is a beloved figure among customers of all ages. He is often seen smiling and waving at guests, making their visit to our restaurant a memorable one.

Chica

Chica is our backup singer of the band here at Freddy Fazbear's Pizza. She is known her cheerful personality. Chica is often seen dancing and interacting with guests, adding a fun and lively atmosphere to our restaurant.

Bonnie

Bonnie is our resident guitarist here at Freddy Fazbear's Pizza. He is known for his energetic personality. Bonnie is often seen singing and performing on stage, bringing excitement and entertainment to our restaurant.

Foxy

Foxy is the pirate captain here at Freddy Fazbear's Pizza. He is known for his mysterious presence. Foxy is often seen standing at Pirate's Cove, bringing a sense of adventure to our restaurant.
